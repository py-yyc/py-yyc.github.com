Meeting agenda template

Meeting agenda is roughly the following:
    6pm doors open. Room setup, chairs, etc.
    6:15: Introduce new members, get everyone setup with wifi, any files they need to download so they can follow along. Directions to bathroom. Thank sponsors.
    6:30: Officially get started with the talks. Aim for 30-90 minutes of presentations.
    ~h:mm: Open time for project nights. Work on personal projects, get help, help others, chat, go home, etc.
    9pm (or whenever host has to leave, whichever is earlier): Wrap up and go home.

Guidelines for presenters

Provide a synopsis of your talk. Roughly how time required, targetted audience, prerequisite knowledge, setup, and skill level. Upload any materials. Request special equipment. Speak loudly because of fan projector. Projector supports DVI, HDMI and adapters are provided for Macbooks.
